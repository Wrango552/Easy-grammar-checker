Auto-fix rules

  • Remove duplicate spaces and spaces before punctuation.
  • Fix repeated words like the the.
  • Capitalize the first word of a sentence.
  • Repair common contractions such as dont, cant, and theyre.
  • Keep grammar judgment calls as suggestions only.

Why it stays basic

A lot of grammar tools try to sound smarter by rewriting whole sentences. This one avoids that so your draft still sounds like you.
